Welcome to the Robotics and Motion Labratory at the University of Illinois at Chicago

We are interested in developing robotic prototypes and practical control algorithms that would push the state-of-the-art of current systems. We use prototyping tools such as 3D printers and simulations and tools in system identification, optimization, feedback control, and learning. Please see videos for a sampling of the systems we work on.

There are no openings for PhD students at this time. Current UIC MS students considering Thesis option are welcome to join. UIC undergraduate students (any engineering or CS major) are welcome to join. Contact pranav AT uic DOT edu (pranav@uic.edu).

Lab Location

The RAM lab is located in 1032W Engineering Research Facility (ERF). The closest parking garage is the  Halsted/Taylor Parking Structure 760 W Taylor St, Chicago, IL 60607.
